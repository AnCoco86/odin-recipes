Spaghetti & Meatballs

This is a favorite dish of mine. Combines ground beef mixed with spices and aromatics, and seared. The Fond and Fat are then used to sweat more aromatics in the form of garlic, and chili flakes. This becomes the base for a rich tomato sauce, which will be finished with whole leaves of basil.

Ingredients

  • grated parmesan cheese
  • 1# Ground Beef
  • 1/2# Italian Sausage
  • 1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 whole egg
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1 bunch basil

Steps:

  1. combine meats, cheese, garlic, knead together
  2. add in panko and 1 egg; knead until well combined, and a bit tacky
  3. scoop out meatballs, and roll by hand onto a plate
  4. Heat olive oil in a dutch oven or large saute pan;
  5. Sear the meatballs, about 2 minutes per side; remove to the side
  6. without draining the fat, add sliced garlic and saute 1-2 minutes.
  7. add in crushed tomatoes; add in meatballs
  8. allow sauce to rise to a simmer; this will cook 10 minutes
  9. (begin your pasta)
  10. about 5 minutes in, add a bunch of basil directly to the sauce
  11. drop heat to very low, taste sauce for seasoning; should be thickened and delicious. Remove basil, and throw out.
  12. Cook pasta al dente, and toss with the sauce;
  13. Finish with fresh basil and cheese. Enjoy!
