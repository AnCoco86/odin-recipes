Butter Chicken

This is a simple, inexpensive preparation on classic indian Butter Chicken. It removes some of the steps, and therefore some of labor and cost, while still delivering tons of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2# boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 8 oz whole fat yogurt
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • kosher salt
  • 2" knob of ginger, brunoised
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 14 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 T garam masala
  • 1 1/2 t tumeric

Steps:

  1. cut chicken into 1" pieces; marinade by combining yogurt, 1 tsp garam masala, and kosher salt
  2. let sit 30 minutes - overnight
  3. chop onion, brunoise ginger; set aside
  4. Heat olive oil in a dutch oven or large saute pan;
  5. in batches, sear the chicken; put aside
  6. sweat onion, ginger 3-4 minutes; add 1 T of water to help free the fond
  7. add the spices; toast gently 1-2 minutes
  8. add in crushed tomatoes, along with 1 cup of water
  9. simmer sauce 5-8 minutes, until thickened; blend with hand blender to smooth if desired
  10. return chicken to the pan; simmer another 3-5 minutes to cook through
  11. add 1 cup heavy cream; simmer 3-4 minutes on low
  12. mount the pan with 2 T unsalted butter, stirred in. Serve over steamed rice
